Mission

4est is dedicated to providing a non-partisan community for review of online journalism.

Rating System

Our metric takes into account the structural aspects of journalism.

Contributing

You can post your own reviews by creating an account, or you can assist in development by contributing on Github here: https://github.com/johnyatesiv/4est

Appeal Process

The author of a piece may contest a rating given by the community. In this case, the appeal will be submitted to an administrator for review. Individual reviews may also be flagged by the community for terms violations. If your review has been flagged, you may appeal to an administrator.
